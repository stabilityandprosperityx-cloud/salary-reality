## Local software engineering salaries: Barcelona vs Madrid (typical bands)

For **Spanish employment contracts**, software engineering compensation is usually stated as **annual gross** in euros. Directional 2026 recruiter conversations often look like:

- **Junior:** roughly **€30,000–€42,000** gross per year in many product companies (varies by city and stack).
- **Mid-level:** **€42,000–€55,000** gross per year for common backend/frontend/full-stack roles in Madrid and Barcelona.
- **Senior:** **€55,000–€75,000+** gross per year for strong performers, with outliers higher for specialized cloud/security roles.

Smaller cities may trend lower, while certain international HQs may pay premiums.

## Remote software engineers living in Spain

Remote engineers paid by US or UK companies frequently report higher **total cash** than local Spanish payroll, especially for senior roles. However, structuring matters: some workers remain on foreign payroll, others use employer-of-record entities in Spain. Each structure changes compliance, contributions, and net outcomes.

## Gross vs net: Spanish income tax and social contributions (high level)

Net pay depends on personal circumstances and autonomous community rules because **regional income tax** layers onto state taxation. That is why two engineers with the same gross in different regions can see different net. Always use a Spain-specific estimator with your region and family situation.

## Digital Nomad visa: income requirements vs market pay

Spain’s digital nomad pathway is attractive for remote workers, but approval depends on documented income thresholds and contractual evidence. Treat visa minimums as separate from “comfortable salary.” You may qualify legally while still wanting a higher buffer for housing deposits and emergencies.

## FAQ

### Is Barcelona or Madrid better for software engineer salaries?

Both cities show similar broad bands for many product-engineering roles; differences often come from specific employers, languages required, and domain (fintech vs ecommerce).

### Are Spanish salaries lower than German salaries?

Often yes on gross for local contracts, but compare net and rent together. Lifestyle and savings outcomes are not determined by gross alone.

### Do Spanish companies pay a 14-month salary structure?

Some contracts include extra payments across the year; clarify whether advertised gross is **14 payments** or **12 payments** to avoid surprises.

### What should remote workers verify before moving to Spain?

Payroll entity, tax residency plan, healthcare coverage, and whether your employer supports compliant Spanish work authorization pathways.
