## Local salaries vs expat remote salaries

- **Local employment** in Tbilisi often pays far less in GEL than remote workers earn in USD/EUR from abroad.
- **Remote workers** may keep global pay scales while living locally, dramatically changing spending power.
- Averages on national statistics websites may **underrepresent** expat remote earners if they are not captured the same way.

## Virtual Zone IT companies

Georgia’s IT “Virtual Zone” concept is frequently referenced by founders and contractors. It can be advantageous for qualifying IT services exports, but setup and compliance are not casual. Engage a local accountant if you are serious.

## Cost of living in Tbilisi

Rent, dining, and transport can be affordable compared to Western Europe, but imported goods, cars, and premium housing can be pricey. Budget realistically for winter utilities and air quality mitigation if needed.
